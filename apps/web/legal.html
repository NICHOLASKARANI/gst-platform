GST Legal & Privacy Compliance

⚠️ Kenya Data Protection Act 2019

GST's consent-based tracking features are fully compliant with the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019.

All location tracking requires EXPLICIT, INFORMED CONSENT from the individual being tracked.

🔐 How Consent Works

  1. Requester sends a consent request via phone number
  2. Target receives a unique consent link
  3. Target explicitly approves or denies the request
  4. Only after approval does tracking begin
  5. Target can revoke consent at ANY time
  6. Consent automatically expires after 7 days

📱 Legitimate Use Cases

  • Family location sharing (parent-child)
  • Fleet management (employee devices)
  • Emergency response (authorized personnel)
  • Asset tracking (company property)

🚫 PROHIBITED USES

  • Tracking individuals without consent
  • Stalking or harassment
  • Unauthorized surveillance
  • Violation of privacy rights

Violations may result in legal prosecution under Kenyan law.
